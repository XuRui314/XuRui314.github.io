ted-learning-3 clues to understanding your brain

Summary

The function of structures of human brain.

How to study the brain scinence.

The main approach is to look at patients with sustained damage to a small region of the brain. Then you get a highly selected loss in one function with other functions being preserved intact. And this just give the confidence of asserting that region of brain is somehow involved in mediating that function. To put it simply again, after amputated the arm, the learned paralysis carries over into his body image, the patient still feel the pain from the phantom.

And through the visual input, he now get the right feeling of his phantom, and gradually get rid of this kind of phantom.
